FAQ

What is the minimum order quantity? +
Our minimum wholesale order is 10 cases (120 bottles). For orders above 100 cases, you unlock free bonus cases.
Where do you deliver? +
We deliver across all major regions in Tanzania, including Dar es Salaam, Arusha, Dodoma, and Mwanza. Logistics fees may apply for remote areas.
What are the payment terms? +
Payment must be settled in full before dispatch for new partners. We accept Bank Transfers, Mobile Money (M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa), and corporate checks.
What is the lead time for an order? +
Standard wholesale orders are processed and dispatched within 3-5 business days. Large scale container orders require 2 weeks notice.
