Vin Nkolo Mboka

Born from Tanzania's fertile highlands, Nkolo Mboka blends the fiery warmth of fresh ginger root with the sweetness of wild honey — a bold, warming spirit unlike anything else on the continent.

"Nkolo Mboka" captures the Swahili spirit of rising, of emergence — a drink that lifts you like the lion that defines our land.
